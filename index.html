About Us

Welcome to Grand Hotel & Suites, where luxury meets comfort. Established in 1990, our hotel has been a beacon of hospitality, offering guests an unforgettable experience with world-class amenities and personalized service.

Nestled in the heart of the city, we provide easy access to major attractions while offering a tranquil retreat. Our dedicated team is committed to making your stay as enjoyable and memorable as possible, whether you're traveling for business or leisure.

Experience the difference with our elegant rooms, exquisite dining, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that makes you feel right at home.

Where We Are

The Grand Hotel & Suites is located in the heart of Val di Non, in the hamlet of Castelfondo in Borgo d'Anaunia (TN).
An ideal place for those seeking relaxation, nature and culture among apple orchards, canyons and castles.

What to do during your holiday

Experience Val di Non between nature, sports, and outdoor adventures, in every season.

Skiing and Snowshoeing

Skiing and snowshoeing amidst majestic snowy landscapes. Slopes for all levels, ideal for the whole family, in nearby resorts like Passo Mendola, Val d'Ultimo, Folgarida. Equipment rental available in the area.

Learn more →
Canyoning

Adventure through gorges and waterfalls with expert guides along the picturesque Novella. Accompanied by expert guides, you will traverse spectacular gorges, natural slides, and waterfalls in complete safety. A perfect activity for those seeking adrenaline and nature.

Learn more →
Hiking

Panoramic walks among apple orchards, alpine lakes, and mountain huts in the Maddalene. The owner, an expert of the territory, is available for advice and guided tours by reservation. Experiences suitable for the whole family, with the possibility of reaching the mountain by car (paid service).

Learn more →
Novella River Park

A fascinating route through canyons, walkways, and unspoiled nature. Also to be explored by kayak with expert guides. Ideal for all ages.
