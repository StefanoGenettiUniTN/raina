Raina: a little corner waiting to be discovered

Our B&B was born from the joy of meeting and welcoming new people—different, nearby, and even from faraway countries and cultures.

People who leave a mark on our hearts, people who return, people you may never see again!

That's the beauty of a dream come true: to travel—and when you can't travel, open your doors to those who can do it for you!

Our Family

Each of us—Nadia, Ruben, and Anna—had fun designing and decorating "our own" room based on what we liked most.

We left the finishing touches to Grandma Maria Teresa, from the crochet embroidery to the little details that make our B&B cozy and unique.

Bed & Breakfast

A little handmade world
In every room and in the breakfast area, you'll find something of ours—designed and crafted by our own hands.
From wood to fabric, from photos to puzzles: the material doesn't matter. The beauty of decorating a home is letting it reflect who you are through the things you create!

Raina House Apartments

After the B&B, we thought of those who, for various reasons, prefer independence and autonomy.
So our goal was to create three apartments for holidays—or simply for those who want to escape the everyday routine!
We named each apartment after places in our mountains—spectacular locations where Mother Nature gifts us silence, scents, and colors.

Sous

Located on the ground floor, it is the largest and most characteristic apartment, furnished by us with fir wood from the mountain areas of our village. It features two bedrooms, a bathroom, and a bright living area with a kitchenette and sofa bed. Next door and independent is the Sordin: a double room with its own bathroom. It’s ideal for families or groups.

What to Do During Your Holiday

Experience Val di Non through nature, sports, and outdoor adventures in every season.

Skiing and Snowshoeing

Skiing and snowshoeing among majestic snowy landscapes. Slopes for all skill levels, perfect for the whole family, in nearby resorts like Passo Mendola, Val d'Ultimo, and Folgarida. Equipment rental available in the area.

Find out more →
Canyoning

Adventure through gorges and waterfalls with expert guides along the stunning Novella stream. You'll cross dramatic canyons, natural slides, and waterfalls in total safety. A perfect activity for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

Find out more →
Hiking

Scenic walks among apple orchards, alpine lakes, and mountain lodges in the Maddalene range. The host, a local expert, is available for advice and guided tours (upon request). Family-friendly experiences, with car access to the mountains available (paid service).

Find out more →
Novella River Park

A fascinating route through canyons, walkways, and pristine nature. Also explorable by kayak with experienced guides. Ideal for both adults and children.
