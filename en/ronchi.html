Ronchi Apartment

Overlooking the orchards, Ronchi tells stories of blooming apple trees and summer cherries.

A bright apartment where nature flows in through the windows, and every day carries the scent of slowly ripening seasons.

Ronchi

Ronchi is the name of an area north of our small village, where in recent years a water reservoir has been built for irrigating the fruit trees, including apple and cherry trees. It's definitely one of the highlights to visit!

The apartment, whose windows face this area, features a double bedroom, a bathroom, and a living area with a kitchenette and sofa bed.
