Breakfast Room

A sweet awakening surrounded by exquisite local aromas.

Here, we combined our efforts and let our creativity run wild! We restored our family's old carpenter's workbench, which now serves as the centerpiece for our fantastic homemade breakfasts made with local products.

You won't find the usual ham, but rather delicious speck and special cheese from the shops in our village of Castelfondo. While Thun cups enhance the flavor of your coffee, a rustic early 20th-century display cabinet adds a cozy, intimate touch.
